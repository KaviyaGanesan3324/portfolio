Featured Project

Rockfall Prediction & Alarm System

A real-time safety system engineered to detect and predict potential rockfall or landslide events in mountainous terrains.

The system leverages an array of environmental sensors interfaced with microcontrollers to continuously monitor geological conditions including vibration, tilt, moisture, and seismic activity.

Upon detecting abnormal thresholds, the system triggers immediate alert notifications via GSM/Wi-Fi modules enabling timely evacuation and accident prevention.

Technologies Used:

  • Arduino / ESP32
  • Vibration, Tilt & Moisture Sensors
  • GSM / Wi-Fi Communication Modules
