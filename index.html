Featured Project

Rockfall Prediction & Alarm System

A real-time safety system engineered to detect and predict potential rockfall or landslide events in mountainous terrains. The system leverages environmental sensors interfaced with microcontrollers to monitor vibration, tilt, moisture, and seismic activity. Upon detecting abnormal thresholds, it triggers immediate alerts via GSM/Wi-Fi modules.

  • Microcontrollers: Arduino / ESP32
  • Sensor Array: Vibration, Tilt & Moisture Sensors
  • Communication: GSM / Wi-Fi Alert Modules
